Insight-Led Regional Marketing

Using data and audience insight to improve campaign effectiveness and engagement.

Key Result: Increased engagement and expanded audience reach through multi-channel campaign delivery.
Key Impact: Delivered integrated campaigns that significantly increased awareness, engagement, and reach across digital and community channels.

The Challenge

  • Limited visibility on campaign performance and audience behaviour.
  • Need to deliver consistent messaging across a regional network.
  • Balancing multiple stakeholders and priorities.

The Approach

  • Used analytics tools to monitor website and campaign performance.
  • Optimised content based on user behaviour and engagement data.
  • Supported teams with guidance on branding and communications.
  • Delivered targeted campaigns aligned to audience needs.

Impact

  • Improved campaign performance and reporting.
  • Stronger consistency in brand and messaging.
  • Increased engagement across digital platforms.
  • Better understanding of audience needs and behaviours.
