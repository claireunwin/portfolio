Hospice Awareness Campaigns

Marketing & Communications Strategy

The Challenge

Increase public awareness and engagement around hospice care and end-of-life conversations through national awareness campaigns.

The Strategy

  • Coordinate multi-channel communications
  • Deliver PR activity and media engagement
  • Develop digital content across social and web
  • Produce publications for supporters and health professionals

Execution

  • Managed campaigns including Dying Matters Week and Hospice Care Week
  • Worked with leadership team to align messaging with organisational goals
  • Delivered supporter magazine and health sector newsletters
  • Coordinated press responses and PR opportunities

Results

Improved awareness of hospice care services and strengthened engagement with supporters, partners and the wider community.
