Hospice Awareness Campaigns

Led multi-channel marketing campaigns to increase awareness of hospice care, coordinating digital communications, PR activity, and publications.

The Challenge

Hospice services often struggle to raise awareness and encourage engagement around sensitive topics. The goal was to increase awareness while maintaining a compassionate tone.

The Strategy

Multi-channel campaigns
Digital engagement across web and social
Local media and stakeholder relations
Accessible communications for all audiences

Results

Increased awareness and understanding of hospice services
Stronger engagement with supporters and stakeholders
Expanded digital communications across platforms
Improved visibility in the local community
