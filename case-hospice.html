Hospice Awareness Campaigns

Led multi-channel marketing campaigns to increase awareness of hospice care, coordinating digital communications, PR activity, and publications.

The Challenge

  • Hospice services needed increased awareness in the community.
  • Communication around sensitive topics had to remain compassionate.
  • Reaching diverse audiences across digital and traditional channels.

The Strategy

  • Implemented multi-channel marketing campaigns.
  • Created digital content for web, email, and social media.
  • Coordinated press releases and stakeholder communications.
  • Adapted content for accessibility and audience preferences.

Results

  • Increased public awareness of hospice services.
  • Improved engagement with supporters and stakeholders.
  • Expanded digital communications footprint.
  • Positive media coverage and community recognition.
