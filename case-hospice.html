Hospice Awareness Campaigns

Delivering multi-channel campaigns to increase awareness and engagement around hospice care.

The Challenge

  • Low public awareness and understanding of hospice services.
  • Sensitive subject matter requiring careful, empathetic messaging.
  • Need to engage multiple audience groups across channels.

The Approach

  • Developed integrated campaigns across digital, print, and PR.
  • Created audience-focused messaging tailored to different demographics.
  • Worked closely with internal teams to ensure consistency and accuracy.
  • Delivered content across web, email, social media, and publications.

Impact

  • Increased awareness and understanding of hospice services locally.
  • Improved engagement with supporters and stakeholders.
  • Expanded reach across digital channels.
  • Strengthened community presence and visibility.
