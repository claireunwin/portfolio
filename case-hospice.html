Hospice Awareness Campaigns

Leading multi-channel campaigns to increase awareness, engagement and community understanding of hospice care.

Key Impact: Delivered integrated campaigns that significantly increased awareness, engagement, and reach across digital and community channels.

The Challenge

  • Low awareness and understanding of hospice care within the local community.
  • Sensitive subject matter requiring careful, empathetic messaging.
  • Need to engage diverse audiences across digital and offline channels.

The Strategy

  • Led integrated campaigns including Dying Matters Week and Hospice Care Week.
  • Developed audience-first messaging tailored to different demographics.
  • Aligned digital, PR, print and internal communications for consistency.
  • Worked cross-functionally to ensure messaging accuracy and impact.

Execution

  • Delivered content across social media, email, website and publications.
  • Managed press enquiries and external communications.
  • Oversaw campaign planning, timelines and performance tracking.

Results

  • Significant increase in engagement across digital channels.
  • Expanded reach and visibility within the local community.
  • Strengthened public understanding of hospice services.
  • Improved internal alignment and campaign effectiveness.
