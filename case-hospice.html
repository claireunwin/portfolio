Hospice Awareness Campaigns

Organisation: Ashgate Hospice

Role: Marketing & Communications Manager

Overview

Led communications and marketing campaigns to increase awareness of hospice care services and encourage open conversations around end-of-life care. Campaigns included nationally recognised awareness weeks and community engagement initiatives.

The Challenge

Hospice services often face challenges in raising awareness and encouraging public engagement around sensitive topics such as end-of-life care. The objective was to increase awareness of the organisation’s services while maintaining a respectful and compassionate tone.

The Strategy

  • Deliver coordinated multi-channel campaigns
  • Increase digital engagement across social and web platforms
  • Strengthen relationships with local media and stakeholders
  • Develop accessible communications to reach diverse audiences

Execution

  • Led campaigns including national hospice awareness initiatives
  • Coordinated press responses and media engagement
  • Produced supporter publications and newsletters
  • Collaborated with internal teams to align campaign messaging
  • Delivered digital and print content across multiple channels

Results

  • Improved awareness and understanding of hospice services
  • Strengthened engagement with supporters and stakeholders
  • Expanded digital communications across web and social platforms
  • Increased visibility of hospice campaigns within the local community
